RAK Free Zone Company Formation

Ras Al Khaimah Free Trade Zone (RAK FTZ) is one of the best emerging free zones in the Emirates which houses over 8000 companies across 50 industry sectors. Strategic location, excellent connectivity with the emerging markets, cost-effective entrepreneurial environment, ease of trans-shipment, and lucrative tax incentives are some of the reasons which make RAK free trade zones an ideal destination for company setup.

Maven business consultancy has vast experience in availing business licenses for foreign investors, non-residents, and foreign companies from the RAK investment authority and helping them with hassle-free business setup in Ras Al Khaimah.


Type of Companies That Can be Registered in RAK Free Zones

Companies interested in RAK free zone business setup can register in any of the RAK free zones as:

  • RAK Free Zone Company: owned by 2 to 5 partners
  • RAK FTZ Local Branch: branch of a domestic firm in UAE
Business Licenses Required for RAK Free Zone Company Setup

RAK free zone company formation requires companies to avail any of the following trade licenses:

  • RAK FTZ Commercial License
  • RAK FTZ Industrial License
Free Zones in RAK

RAK free zones are the world-class trading hubs which provide state-of-the-art infrastructure facilities like land,warehouses,and office space to meet diverse investor requirements of small, medium,and large businesses.

At Maven, we can help you form your company in any of the following free zones:

  • Ras Al Khaimah Business Park
  • Ras Al Khaimah Industrial Park (RAK FTZ Industrial park)
  • Ras Al Khaimah Technology Park
  • Ras Al Khaimah Academic Zone

Why Opt for RAK Free Zone Company Formation?

Offering the least rates as compared to any other emirate, Ras Al Khaimah is a cherished company formation destination for companies trying to gain a foothold in the UAE. RAK free zone company setup offers unmatched incentives to investors, like:

  • 100% foreign ownership and repatriation of capital and profits
  • Advanced communication facilities
  • Cost-effective business setup
  • Exemption from import and export duties
  • Fast-track immigration process
  • Flexible and transparent business laws
  • Flexible working hours
  • Low cost of land and living
  • No restrictions on currency or recruiting labor
  • Simple staffing and recruitment procedures
  • Simplified company incorporation process
