Type of Companies That Can be Registered in Sharjah Free Zones

Sharjah free zone company formation and registration is hassle-free. Based on the type of shareholding, two types of companies can be registered in Sharjah Free Zones:

  • FZE or Free Zone Establishment can be formed by a single shareholder
  • FZC or Free Zone Company can be formed with 2 to 5 shareholders. It is a multiple shareholder limited liability company


Licenses Required for Sharjah Free Zone Company Setup

In order to form a company and practice business in free zones, investors or expatriates need to avail following types of Sharjah business licenses:

  • Commercial License
  • General Trading License
  • Industrial / Professional License

The first step to avail Sharjah Free Zone License is to select the type of business activity to be performed, followed by selecting a unique name for your company and ensuring that it’s not already registered with the chosen Free Zone.


Free Zones in Sharjah

There are 2 free zones in Sharjah which permit 100% foreign ownership. Team at Maven Business Consultancy is aware of all the laws and regulations of business setup in Sharjah free zones, and can help you form your company in your chosen free zone with ease.

  • Hamriyah Free Zone: Established in 1995, this tax haven has excellent connectivity with all seven emirates and neighboring Gulf states, and provides competitive incentives to foreign investors making it a lucrative investment hub. At Maven, we take care of the entire business setup procedures, documentation, registration, and licensing procedures to help businesses avail all privileges of this free trading zone.
  • Sharjah Airport International Free Zone (SAIF Zone): Also established in 1995, SAIF Zone is a premier hub for companies and trading establishments, thanks to its proximity to the Sharjah International Airport. Strategically located, it has excellent air, sea, and land connectivity, making it most efficient free zone for business operations. It’s houses 4500+ commercial companies & industries, and attracts consumers from over 91 countries.
