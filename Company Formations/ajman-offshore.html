Ajman Offshore

Located centrally at one of the most strategic locations in the United Arab Emirates, at the doorway of the Arabian Gulf, Ajman Offshore has become a key investment destination for foreign companies and expatriates. Regulated by the Ajman Free Zone Authority (AFZA), under the Ajman Offshore Companies Regulations of 2014, Ajman Offshore is well-placed to cater to both Eastern and Western markets, thanks to its excellent connectivity with Ajman seaport and the Gulf coast. Additionally, inexpensive business setup and lucrative tax incentives make Ajman offshore company formation a lucrative business decision for foreign investors.

Shareholders

At least one shareholder is required for Ajman offshore company registration and has the choice to decide the capital structure of the company. Corporate shareholders are allowed.

Directors

At least two directors are required for Ajman offshore company setup. A shareholder(s) can also act as the companyâ€™s director, but corporate directors are not acceptable.

Secretary

The director's role as secretary must be formally documented in the company's records . This dual responsibility streamlines administrative processes and facilitates efficient communication.

Local Requirements

Confidentiality

The details of shareholders & directors supplied to the Ajman authorities remain confidential and are not disclosed to public.

Time Required for Company Formation

Permissible Activities

Investors can engage in any of the following activities in Ajman Offshore:

  • Commission Agents Company or Intermediary Brokers (IB's)
  • Consulting and Advisory Services
  • General Trading
  • Holding Company â€“ Buying, holding stakes, or selling in companies
  • International Services
  • Investments and Joint Investments Company

Documents Required

Following documents are required from the shareholders to form a company in Ajman Offshore:

  • Original bank reference letter
  • Original residence proof
  • Passport copies
  • Shareholder resume or profile
  • Desired offshore company name
  • Valid UAE Visa or Entry Stamp page copy
  • Information about the number of shares each shareholder holds

Limitations

An Ajman offshore company cannot:

  • Acquire tax residency certificate
  • Carry out business or engage in any activity outside the Free Zone and within the UAE (however, they can have business deal with companies in Ajman Free Zone)
  • Get UAE residence visa for the shareholder(s)
  • Have a physical office in the United Arab Emirates
  • Own more than three properties

Advantages of Ajman Offshore Company Formation

Here are some reasons why global investors opt for Ajman Offshore business setup:

  • 100% corporate and personal tax exemption
  • 100% foreign ownership
  • 100% repatriation of capital and profit
  • Complete ownership of your intellectual property - copyrights, patents, trademarks, etc.
  • Complete privacy, confidentiality and protection of assets
  • Fast and cost-effective registration process
  • Flexibility to open a bank account in UAE
  • Flexibility to own AFZA approved freehold properties while availing benefits of 47 UAE double tax treaties
  • Less setup costs as compared to onshore companies
  • Minimum capital requirement
  • No foreign currency restrictions
  • No need to register with the Chamber of Commerce
  • No need to rent an industrial premise or an office
