Free zone Company Formation in Dubai

Dubai is not only a popular tourism destination with ultramodern skyscrapers, luxury shopping getaways, and lively nightlife, but is equally renowned for special economic free zones providing various lucrative benefits to expatriates and investors.

State-of-the-art business and telecommunications infrastructure, inexpensive workforce, easy recruitment policies, cosmopolitan lifestyle, government support, and no professional or personal taxes are just a few rewards foreign nationals get to open a company in Dubai free zone. Free zone company formation in Dubai is, therefore, a lucrative business opportunity for global investors.

Type of Companies That Can be registered in Dubai Free Zones

There are predefined legal requirements and guidelines which need to be fulfilled to register a business entity in any free zone. Maven is a leading business consultancy in UAE, and takes into consideration various factors like short-term and long-term company goals, investment capital, and cost & legal structure before suggesting you the right jurisdiction.

Depending on your business type and shareholder details, we can help you register your company as either of the following:

Free Zone Establishment (FZE)

single-shareholder establishment (either an individual person, corporate shareholder, or a company)

Free Zone Company (FZCO)

Requires 2 or more shareholders to be registered (either individual, corporate shareholders, or companies)

Branch Office or Representative Office

An extension of the foreign parent company having the same trading license as its parent company


Type of Licenses Available in Dubai Free Zones

Depending on your business type and requirements, the team at Maven will help you obtain any of the following types of business licenses in a Free Zone of your choice.

  • General Trading License: This type of license allows companies to trade in general goods. Products which are banned or need special approvals are exempted under this license
  • Trading License: Also known as commercial license, it allows EFZ companies to perform all types of trading activities like buying, selling, and importing / exporting goods and commodities
  • Service License: Companies which offer services like accountancy, consultancy, etc. are accorded with this type of license
  • Professional License: This license is given to companies offering professional services, like HR management, PR relations, management consultancy, etc.
  • Industrial License: Companies which carry out activities or businesses like manufacturing, goods processing, or product packaging are provided with Industrial license
