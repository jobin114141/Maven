Why UAE

The United Arab Emirates’ remarkable journey dates back to 1892. Given its tax environment, world class infrastructure, low tariff and 100% ownership for foreign investors in selected zones, the U. A. E is the ideal location for setting up new business. Been a financial hub, U. A. E stands as a strong nation built on the foundation of security, economic strength and gives your company access to the emerging markets of the Middle East, North Africa, and the Indian sub-continent, an area that includes over 30 countries and 1.8 billion people.

Foreign investors looking to set up business in any of the industrial cities, special economic zones or free trade zones established in different emirates promise quick turnaround in terms of registration and visa processing.
